Sterling Shroud

"Sterling Shroud is a point-and-click atmospheric horror game with a focus on navigation and puzzle solving. Can you escape the forest alive?"

This game is a 2D point-and-click horror game that sees you navigating a dark forest in an attempt to escape, or at least uncover the truth about what is holding you there. While solving classic point-and-click puzzles and reading notes left behind by others, you navigate the forest using a map that you manage!

This was the first game I published on Steam! I really buckled down with this project and focused on polish and professionalism. What I'm most proud of with this game is the map the player fills in themselves and the puzzles that go hand-in-hand with that feature.

I became very familiar with the process of playtesting while working on this project. Collecting feedback from testers helped me fashion a more intuitive, fun, and interesting game. Managing an expansive dialogue system was also a new challenge for me, but one that I overcame with time and experience.
