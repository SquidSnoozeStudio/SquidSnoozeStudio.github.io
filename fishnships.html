Fish n' Ships

"Take to the seas on your little boat and catch some fish in this 2D cozy fishing experience! Explore the drizzly coastline, upgrade your boat, and bring home the big one! How many fish can you catch?"

Made in just under two months, this game is a 2D fishing simulator focused on casting and manipulating your line, catching and selling fish, and upgrading your boat. It features multiple fishing spots with exclusive fish, progression unlocks, and even a mysterious story!

This was a remake of one of my first-ever game development projects. My skills have come a long way since then, so I thought it would be fun to breath some new life into an old game of mine.

I learned a lot about UI design with all the menus I created in this game. I also worked hard on the balance and progression of the game, with a focus on pacing and how the player progression ties into the story.
