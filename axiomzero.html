Axiom Zero

"In this short game, you will experience the growth of a small robot after it finds something precious it needs to protect."

This was my entry into the GMTK Game Jam 2024. Having only four days to work on this game, I had to keep the visuals and mechanics limited. The game itself is a narrative-driven experience where you play as a little robot collecting materials. By deconstructing and destroying the environment you're playing in, you grow incrementally larger and destroy more levels as you go.

This was my first attempt at weaving a narrative into a video game. I paid special attention to the music and visuals, and how that affects the mood as the player experiences the story.

I learned a lot about keeping a project's scope in check while working on this game, as well as managing my time. Every second counts while working on a game jam, and where you direct your effort can radically change the final product. I also worked on figuring out how to manage cut scenes and how they can ease in and out of gameplay for advanced storytelling.
